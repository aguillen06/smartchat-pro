Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take? ▼
Setup takes about 5 minutes. You create your account, add your business information, and customize your AI's greeting. We provision your phone number instantly and you're live. Most businesses are taking calls within 10 minutes of signing up.
Does it work with my existing phone number? ▼
Yes! You can forward your existing number to SmartChat, or we can provide you with a new local number. Call forwarding is the easiest option and works with any phone provider.
Can it handle Spanish callers? ▼
Absolutely! SmartChat is bilingual by default and automatically detects when a caller speaks Spanish. It can also handle Spanglish (code-switching between English and Spanish), which is a huge advantage for businesses serving Hispanic markets.
What happens after my free trial? ▼
After 14 days, you'll be prompted to choose a paid plan. If you don't upgrade, your SmartChat will be paused (not deleted). You can upgrade anytime to resume service. No surprise charges.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, cancel anytime from your dashboard. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You own all your call data and can export it before canceling.
